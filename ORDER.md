The teams are ranked in the league table by points gained, then goal difference,
then goals scored, and then their head-to-head record for that season (including
away goals record). If two or more teams finish the season equal in all these
respects, then teams are separated by alphabetical order, unless a promotion,
relegation, or play-off place (see below) is at stake, when the teams are
separated by a play-off game, though this improbable situation has never arisen
in all the years the rule has existed.
